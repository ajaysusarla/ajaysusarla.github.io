Last evening, Mumbai (and the entire nation) faced yet another Terror attack. More lives lost. Much more mentally scarred, for life. Of late terrorist attacks in our country have been dangerously on the rise. So much so that there is a Wikipedia entry listing the 'Chronology of the Major Events'.
Such incidents spread uneasiness and fear among all of us. And the obviousness of it was seen on Bangalore streets today. At around 8.30 PM, on my way back home, i noticed quite unusually the traffic, or the lack of it on the roads. People preferred to stay indoors i guess. Or maybe it was just the drizzle.
May the souls of the victims rest in peace. And may God give all those who had go through the traumatizing experience, strength to move on.

Peace.
